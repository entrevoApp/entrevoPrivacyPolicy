Information We Collect
We only collect limited personal data to provide our services effectively:

3. Password – stored securely through Firebase Authentication.

6. Potential Future Analytics Data – while we do not currently collect analytics information, we may do so in the future to help us understand how users engage with the App.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (e.g., racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, health data, etc.). No method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
