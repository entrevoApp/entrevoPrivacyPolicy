Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your Entrevo account, you have two options:

1. From within the app:
Go to the settings section and select "Delete Account." This will immediately remove your account and associated data.

2. By email:
Send us a deletion request at entrevoapp@gmail.com using the same email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 5 business days.

If you have any issues, feel free to contact us.
